What We Offer

MIMETECT is an architectural and interior design company. The company works towards bringing the desired style/design of an individual to a space. MIMETECT also brings together all tools and materials necessary for reconstructing and refurbishing a space to suit the individuals preferred style/design taking into account the functionality of the space. The company partners with building constructors right from the genesis of a project to the end of the project

Our Vision

Pursue to make rawness and refinement complementary features to populate a luxe environment nationwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make every space functional by creating the most efficient and quality layout, taking into account the desired style/design of every individual.

I am delighted to be introducing the mission, vision and goal of MIMETECT. Since starting in this role, it has become increasingly clear to me that while the interior design sector remains an attractive sector, it is entering a period of significant change bringing both challenges and opportunities. In addition, despite improved delivery in recent years, it is also clear there are several areas of spaces nationwide that need to be improved with a touch of interior design by a professional like myself to make spaces more functional instead of looking at just the beauty of a space. That is why I set out the priorities which everyone in the company is focused on. These priorities are; educating our clients on the functions of every item brought into a space and making known to our clients the advantages and disadvantages of the items they prefer to be brought into their space. It is my dream that this reputable company will not only work for clients but also educate clients on all items they choose for a space.
